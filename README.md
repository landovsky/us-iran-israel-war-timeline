# US–Israel–Iran War Timeline

An interactive timeline tracking the 2026 US–Israel war on Iran — from the opening strikes on February 28 through ongoing diplomatic and military developments.

Each event is colour-coded by direction: **escalating**, **de-escalating**, or **neutral**, and tagged by actor (US, Israel, Iran, or other parties). Events include a short description and a primary source link.

**Live site:** https://landovsky.github.io/us-iran-israel-war-timeline/

## What it covers

- Military strikes, naval engagements, and ground operations
- Diplomatic initiatives, ceasefire attempts, and international reactions
- Economic fallout: oil prices, Hormuz closure, energy market disruptions
- Regional spillover: Lebanon, Gulf states, Iraq, Azerbaijan, and beyond

Data is updated manually as significant events occur.
